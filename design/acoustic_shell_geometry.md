# Acoustic Shell Geometry

## 1. Configuration

**Shape**: Cylindrical shell surrounding lithium containment  
**Transducer Mounting**: On outer surface of lithium vessel or on separate acoustic shell

## 2. Dimensions

**Radius**: Matched to lithium vessel (400-600 mm)  
**Height**: 500-800 mm  
**Transducer Positions**: 4-6 vertical rings × 8-12 azimuthal elements = 32-72 total

## 3. Acoustic Modes

**Resonant Frequencies** (cylindrical cavity):
- f_mnl = (c/2π) √[(k_mn/R)² + (lπ/H)²]
- First mode (0,1,0): ~10-15 kHz
- Higher modes: 20-500 kHz

**Control Strategy**: Drive specific modes for stabilization, avoid structural resonances

## 4. Materials

**Acoustic Window**: Thin stainless steel (1-2 mm) for good acoustic transmission  
**Backing Structure**: Rigid frame to support transducers without vibration coupling

## 5. Thermal Isolation

**Challenge**: Transducers must stay <200°C, adjacent to 400-500°C lithium  
**Solution**: Ceramic spacers (10-20 mm alumina) + water-cooled backing plates
