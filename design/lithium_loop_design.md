# Lithium Loop Design

## 1. Baseline Configuration

**Type**: Natural convection (passive)  
**Upgrade Path**: Electromagnetic (EM) pump for forced circulation

## 2. Natural Convection Design

**Driving Force**: Buoyancy from temperature gradients  
**Flow Pattern**: Hot lithium rises near fusion core, cooled lithium descends near heat extractors

**Rayleigh Number Estimate**:
- Ra = gβΔTL³/(να)
- For ΔT=100 K, L=0.5 m, lithium properties at 400°C
- Ra ~ 10⁷ (turbulent natural convection)

**Expected Velocity**: 1-10 cm/s

## 3. EM Pump Option (Phase 2+)

**Type**: DC conduction pump (no moving parts)  
**Principle**: J × B force on conducting fluid

**Specifications**:
- Flow rate: 0.1-1 L/s (0.6-6 m³/hr)
- Pressure head: 1-5 m equivalent (10-50 kPa)
- Power: 100-1000 W
- Efficiency: 10-30%

**Vendor**: Creative Engineers (India), SACOME (France), custom design

## 4. Piping and Valves

**Material**: 316L SS (short term) or Mo-lined  
**Diameter**: 25-50 mm  
**Insulation**: Ceramic fiber, 50-100 mm thick  
**Valves**: None preferred (reliability); if needed, freeze-seal or EM valve

## 5. Heat Exchangers

**Type**: Tube-in-shell or plate heat exchanger  
**Hot side**: Liquid lithium (400-500°C)  
**Intermediate**: Heat pipe (sodium) or direct contact  
**Cold side**: TE modules or water

**Heat Transfer Rate**: 10-100 kW
