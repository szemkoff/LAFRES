# Pressure Vessel Design

## 1. Vacuum Chamber (Fusion Core)

**Design Pressure**: 1 atm external (full vacuum inside)  
**Material**: 316L SS, 3-5 mm thick  
**Geometry**: Spherical, 300-500 mm ID  
**Code**: ASME Section VIII, Division 1

**Stress Analysis**:
- Hoop stress: σ = PR/(2t)
- For R=200 mm, P=101 kPa, t=3 mm: σ ≈ 3.4 MPa
- Allowable stress (316L @ 100°C): ~140 MPa
- Safety factor: >40 (conservative)

**Fabrication**: Hemispheres deep-drawn or spun, TIG welded, X-ray inspected

## 2. Lithium Containment Vessel

**Design Pressure**: 0-2 bar (internal)  
**Material**: 316L SS shell (5-10 mm) + Mo liner (1-2 mm)  
**Geometry**: Cylindrical with dished heads  
**Temperature**: 300-500°C

**Thermal Expansion**:
- ΔL/L = α × ΔT
- For SS, α ≈ 17 × 10⁻⁶ /K
- ΔT = 400 K → ΔL/L ≈ 0.7%
- For 1 m diameter: ΔD ≈ 7 mm

**Accommodation**: Bellows expansion joints or sliding seals

## 3. Testing

**Hydrostatic Test**: 1.5× design pressure (water fill, pressurize)  
**Leak Test**: Helium mass spectrometer, <10⁻⁸ Torr·L/s  
**NDT (Non-Destructive Testing)**: X-ray (welds), ultrasonic (wall thickness), dye penetrant (surface cracks)
