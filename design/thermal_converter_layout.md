# Thermal Converter Layout

## 1. Module Arrangement

**Distribution**: Azimuthally around lithium vessel (8-12 positions)  
**Vertical Stacking**: 2-4 modules per position  
**Total Modules**: 20-50

## 2. Interface Design

**Hot Side**:
- Heat pipe (sodium, 10-20 mm OD) from lithium boundary
- Thermal contact with TE module hot plate (graphite or copper interface)
- Clamping pressure: 1-5 MPa (for good thermal contact)

**Cold Side**:
- Water-cooled cold plate (copper with machined channels)
- Flow rate: 2-5 L/min per module
- Manifold design for parallel flow

## 3. Electrical Connection

**Configuration**: 4-5 modules in series (for voltage), 4-10 parallel strings  
**Output**: ~100-200 VDC → DC-DC converter → 48 VDC  
**Wiring**: High-temp silicone wire (rated 200°C), shielded for EMI

## 4. Maintenance Access

**Modular**: Each TE module independently replaceable  
**Connectors**: Quick-disconnect (thermal and electrical)  
**Replacement Time**: <1 hour per module
