# Sensor Suite Specification

## 1. Temperature Sensors

**Type**: Type K thermocouples (Chromel-Alumel)  
**Operating Range**: 0-500°C  
**Accuracy**: ±1°C or 0.75% (whichever greater)  
**Quantity**: 30-60  
**Locations**: Lithium (10-20), containment walls (5-10), TE modules (10-20), ambient (5-10)  
**Installation**: Thermowells (Mo or SS protective sheaths)

## 2. Neutron Detectors

**Fast Neutrons**:
- Type: Plastic scintillator + PMT
- Sensitivity: 0.1-1 counts per neutron/cm²
- Quantity: 3-5
- Location: Outside lithium blanket, 0.5-2 m from core

**Thermal Neutrons**:
- Type: ³He proportional counter
- Sensitivity: >1000 cps per nv (v=2200 m/s)
- Quantity: 3-5
- Location: Embedded in or near lithium

**Cost**: $5K-$15K per detector

## 3. Acoustic Sensors

**Type**: Piezoelectric pressure transducers (PCB Piezotronics)  
**Frequency Range**: DC to 1 MHz  
**Sensitivity**: 10-100 mV/bar  
**Quantity**: 10-30  
**Mounting**: On containment walls, submersible (high-temp rated)

## 4. Level Sensors

**Type**: Capacitive level sensor  
**Range**: 0-500 mm  
**Resolution**: 0.1-1 mm  
**Quantity**: 2-5  
**Operating Temp**: Up to 200°C (sensor), measure lithium at 400-500°C remotely

## 5. Pressure Sensors

**Cover Gas Pressure**:
- Range: 0-3 bar absolute
- Accuracy: ±0.01 bar
- Type: Capacitance manometer or piezoresistive

**Vacuum Pressure**:
- Range: 10⁻⁸ to 1000 Torr
- Type: Ionization gauge (high vacuum) + Pirani (medium vacuum)

## 6. Electrical Sensors

**Voltage**: High-voltage probes (1000:1 ratio), ±1% accuracy  
**Current**: Rogowski coils or current transformers, ±2% accuracy  
**Power**: Calculated from V×I, logged continuously

## 7. Safety Sensors

**Oxygen Monitor**: 0-25% O₂, alarm at <19.5% or >23.5%  
**Smoke Detector**: Photoelectric, UL listed  
**Radiation Area Monitor**: Neutron rem counter, 0.01-1000 mSv/hr

## 8. Total System Cost

**Estimated**: $30K-$80K for complete sensor suite
