Mission Statement

L.A.F.R.E.S. is a research-grade architecture for a compact neutron-generating system combining:

  • Liquid-lithium blanket for neutron moderation and heat capture
  • Acoustic stabilization for surface control and shock damping
  • Pulsed micro-fusion (non-break-even) for controlled neutron generation
  • Thermoelectric heat extraction for partial energy recovery
  • AI-based control for system optimization and safety

Critical Disclaimer

This project is NOT intended for grid power generation. It is designed for R&D and materials testing only. L.A.F.R.E.S. operates far below the Lawson criterion and does not claim net-positive energy production.

  1. Center: Fusion core chamber (vacuum, 30-50 cm diameter)
  2. Vacuum gap: 5 cm (thermal/electrical isolation)

  5. Acoustic transducers: Mounted on outer lithium boundary
  6. Thermal insulation: Ceramic (2-5 cm)
  7. Containment vessel: Stainless steel with Mo liner (5-10 mm)

Lithium Safety

Fire Prevention & Response

DO NOT USE: Water, CO‚ÇÇ, or standard ABC extinguishers on lithium fires

APPROVED: Class D extinguisher (copper powder, Lith-X‚Ñ¢, dry sand)

Fire Response Procedure:

  1. Evacuate personnel if fire is large
  2. Alert emergency responders (inform of metal fire)
  3. Activate inert gas flood (argon, if available)
  4. Apply Class D agent if safe to approach
  5. Allow to cool before cleanup

Emergency Shutdown (SCRAM)

Automatic Triggers:

  • Lithium temperature >550¬∞C
  • Vacuum loss (P > 10‚Åª¬≥ Torr)
  • Coolant flow failure (<50% nominal)
  • Neutron rate >110% setpoint
  • Radiation monitor >10 mSv/hr
  • Fire/smoke detection
  • Manual SCRAM button

Shutdown Sequence (<1 second):

  1. Disable fusion core high voltage (immediate, <10 ms)
  2. Close deuterium valve (pneumatic, <100 ms)
  3. Activate inert gas purge (if lithium leak detected)
  4. Ramp down acoustic power (5 seconds)
  5. Activate emergency cooling (if over-temperature)
  6. Sound alarms (horn + visual)
  7. Log event (timestamp, trigger, all sensor data)
