# Lithium Handling Specification

## 1. Material Specification

**Lithium Isotope**: Enriched Lithium-7 (⁷Li > 99%)  
**Quantity**: 25 - 100 kg (50 - 200 liters liquid)  
**Purity**: > 99.9% (metals basis), < 100 ppm total impurities  
**Form**: Ingots or pellets for loading

## 2. Procurement

**Suppliers**: FMC Lithium Corporation, Albemarle, SQM  
**Lead Time**: 8-16 weeks for enriched ⁷Li  
**Cost**: $500-1500/kg (enriched), vs. $50-100/kg (natural)  
**Packaging**: Sealed steel containers under argon atmosphere

## 3. Storage

**Container**: Steel drums (30-gallon) with sealed lids  
**Atmosphere**: Argon blanket (&gt;99.99% purity)  
**Location**: Fireproof cabinet or room  
**Temperature**: Ambient (&lt;50°C, well below melting point)  
**Humidity**: Controlled (&lt;20% RH)

## 4. Containment Vessel Specification

| Parameter | Value | Units |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Inner diameter | 400 - 600 | mm |
| Outer diameter | 600 - 800 | mm |
| Height | 500 - 800 | mm |
| Wall material | 316L SS with Mo liner | — |
| Wall thickness | 5-10 (SS) + 1-2 (Mo) | mm |
| Operating temperature | 300 - 500 | °C |
| Maximum temperature (safety) | 550 | °C |
| Cover gas | Argon | — |
| Pressure rating | 0 - 2 | bar |

## 5. Loading Procedure

1. **Prepare vessel**: Clean, evacuate to &lt;10⁻⁴ Torr, backfill with argon  
2. **Transfer lithium**: Through loading port in inert atmosphere (glovebox or argon tent)  
3. **Seal port**: Weld or bolted flange with metal gasket  
4. **Verify seal**: Helium leak test (&lt;10⁻⁸ Torr·L/s)  
5. **Heat to melt**: Gradual ramp (10-20°C/hour) to 200-250°C  
6. **Establish operating temperature**: Continue to 350-450°C

## 6. Operational Monitoring

**Continuous**:
- Temperature (15-30 thermocouples)
- Level (capacitive sensor, ±1 mm)
- Cover gas pressure (0-2 bar)

**Periodic** (weekly):
- Visual inspection (viewport, if available)
- Leak detection (O₂ sensors in enclosure)

**Sampling** (monthly, Phase 2+):
- Extract 5-10 mL sample for chemical analysis (ICP-MS)

## 7. Safety Equipment

**Fire Suppression**: Class D extinguisher (copper powder, salt-based), 10-20 lb capacity  
**Spill Kit**: Dry sand or Lith-X™, sealable containers, non-sparking tools  
**PPE**: Heat-resistant gloves, face shield, fire-resistant clothing  
**Ventilation**: Fume hood or room exhaust (100+ air changes/hour)  
**Detectors**: O₂ monitor (&lt;19.5% alarm), smoke detector, heat detector

## 8. Disposal

**End-of-Life**:
- Cool to &lt;180°C, solidify
- If activated (neutron exposure): Store for decay (weeks-months), dispose as LLRW
- If not activated: React with alcohol (controlled, small batches), neutralize to LiOH, dispose as chemical waste

**Vendor**: Licensed hazardous waste disposal (Clean Harbors, Veolia)
