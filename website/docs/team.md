## Talent Acquisition Strategy

### 1. University Partnerships

- **Faculty:** Part-time consulting or sabbatical assignments
- **Postdocs:** 2-3 year appointments with pathway to permanent positions
- **Graduate Students:** Ph.D./M.S. research projects aligned with project milestones
- **Advantages:** Lower cost, facility access, publication motivation

### 2. National Laboratory Transitions

- **Staff Scientists:** Mid-career researchers seeking entrepreneurial opportunities
- **Retired Personnel:** Experienced staff on short-term or consulting contracts
- **Joint Appointments:** Shared arrangements preserving lab affiliation

### 3. Industry and Private Sector

- **Nuclear Utilities:** Transitioning operators and engineers
- **Fusion Startups:** Personnel from TAE, Commonwealth Fusion, Helion, General Fusion
- **Defense Sector:** Engineers with clearances (valuable for NNSA/DoD collaborations)

### 4. International Recruitment

- **European Programs:** EUROfusion, KIT (Germany), CEA (France)
- **Asia-Pacific:** ITER participants, Japanese and Korean fusion research programs
- **Visa Pathways:** H-1B for specialized roles, EB-1/EB-2 for advanced degree holders
