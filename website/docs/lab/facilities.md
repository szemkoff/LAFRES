# Laboratory Facilities & Equipment

Maximus Energy Corporation operates a fully equipped research laboratory dedicated to fusion energy research and experimental validation.

![Laboratory Overview](/img/lab/facilities-overview.png)
*Complete laboratory setup at Maximus Energy Corporation, Naples, FL.*

## Location

**Address:** 6528 Trail Blvd, Naples, FL 34108

The laboratory provides dedicated space for:
- Fusion reactor experimentation
- Radiation detection and measurement
- Vacuum system testing
- Acoustic and ultrasonic research
- Particle analysis

## Equipment Inventory

### Radiation Detection Systems

| Equipment | Description | Application |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **NaI(Tl) Gamma Detectors** | Wide variety of sodium iodide scintillation detectors | Gamma ray spectroscopy, radiation monitoring |
| **3He Proportional Counters** | Multiple helium-3 neutron detectors | Neutron flux measurement, fusion verification |
| **HPGe Detectors** | Ortec and Canberra high-purity germanium detectors | High-resolution gamma spectroscopy |
| **Tritium Detectors** | Canberra tritium detection systems | Tritium monitoring and safety |
| **X-ray Detectors** | AmpTek x-ray detectors with pulse processors | X-ray spectroscopy, plasma diagnostics |

### Signal Processing & Data Acquisition

| Equipment | Description | Application |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Multi-Channel Analyzers** | Multiple MCA systems | Pulse height analysis, spectroscopy |
| **Digital Oscilloscopes** | Various models | Signal analysis, timing measurements |
| **Bias Supplies** | Ortec and Canberra detector bias | Detector operation |
| **Preamplifiers** | Ortec and Canberra preamps | Signal amplification |
| **Pulse-Shaping Amplifiers** | Ortec and Canberra shaping amps | Signal conditioning |
| **AmpTek Pulse Processors** | Digital pulse processors | X-ray signal processing |

### Vacuum Systems

| **Gauge Controllers** | Electronic controllers | Automated pressure control |
| **Vacuum Parts & Supplies** | Extensive inventory | System maintenance and modification |

## Laboratory Capabilities

### Fusion Research
- Inertial electrostatic confinement (IEC) experiments
- Neutron generation and detection
- Plasma diagnostics
- High voltage discharge studies

### Radiation Measurement
- Gamma spectroscopy (NaI, HPGe)
- Neutron counting (3He proportional counters)
- X-ray spectroscopy (AmpTek)
- Tritium monitoring

### Vacuum Technology
- High vacuum system design and operation
- Residual gas analysis
- Leak detection and system qualification

### Acoustic Research
- Ultrasonic transducer testing
- Acoustic field characterization
- Sonication experiments

### Materials Characterization
- Scanning electron microscopy
- Particle size distribution analysis
- Surface analysis
